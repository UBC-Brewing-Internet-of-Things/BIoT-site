What If Homebrewers Could Automate Brews The Same Way Large Companies Did?

Our team aims to design and implement low-cost data monitoring and develop innovative techniques to brew novel, high-quality beers through various projects.

Meet The Subteams

Instrumentation Team

The instrumentation team is responsible for creating equipment to sense brew data and automate tasks associated with the brewing process.

Brew Team

The brewing team is responsible for designing the brews, maintaining the equipment, and brewing the beer.

Laboratory Team

The lab team is responsible for testing the final product for quality control.
