Join Us!

BIoT is a student-led team dedicated to recreating the industrial brewing process on a home-brewing scale using sensors, automation, and bioengineering. We welcome curious undergraduate students from all backgrounds, especially those interested in brewing, chemical/biological engineering, and computer science - no experience required!

Our 30-40 person team is divided into three sub-teams: brewing and recipe design, bioengineering and assay development, and hardware/software integration. Each year, we tackle new projects, so if you have an idea you want to explore, this is the place to make it happen.

If you’re excited to learn, build, and innovate, join us! Whether you want to develop a new brewing technique, design an experiment, or write software, there’s a place for you here.
